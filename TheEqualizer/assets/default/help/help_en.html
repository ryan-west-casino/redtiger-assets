SPECIAL SYMBOLS AND FEATURES

EXPANDING SLOT

The Equalizer may randomly expand the slot with up to 2 extra reels and 2 extra rows for the current spin.


Wins are always calculated based on the total number of paylines in the game (60) even when the slot is not expanded.

EXPANDING WILDS

After the reels stop, the Equalizer may randomly expand the size of random WILDS on the slot up to 1x6.

WILD SYMBOL

Substitute for all paying symbols.

1x1 WILD symbols may hit on any spin.

Expanded WILDS can be up to 1x6.

FREE SPINS

WILDS that hit during FREE SPINS will remain until the end of FREE SPINS.

Every Slot and WILD Expansion triggered will remain until the end of FREE SPINS.

SOUND WAVE

The speakers in FREE SPINS may randomly send a sound wave across the slot, pushing random WILDS on the screen in the direction of the wave.

+SPIN SCATTER SYMBOL

Awards one extra FREE SPIN when it lands. May only land during FREE SPINS.
