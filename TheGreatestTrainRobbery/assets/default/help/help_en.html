SPECIAL SYMBOLS AND FEATURES

THE BANDITS

Bandit characters may appear to the left or right-hand side of the slot, and once there they may throw a dynamite stick onto the reels.


Dynamite sticks may land on any reel to trigger one of the three Bandit Features.


Two dynamite sticks thrown may trigger the same or different features.

RESPIN

A dynamite explosion may cause a winning respin on two or more reels. It is not possible for bonus symbols to land during a respin. Two dynamite sticks cannot trigger two respins.

CLONING WILDS

Dynamite explosions may start a fire which spreads across the reels, turning symbols into WILDS and guaranteeing a win. The fire may spread in any direction.

MYSTERY SYMBOLS

Smoke from a dynamite explosion may spread across the reels, covering two or more symbols. When the smoke clears, a single symbol type is revealed for a guaranteed win.

FREE SPINS

Triggered when three BONUS symbols land on reels 1, 3 and 5. Each BONUS symbol reveals a random number of FREE SPIN rounds.


During Free Spins the bandits may randomly appear on the slot. If a Bandit appears, he stays for the remaining FREE SPINS and may randomly throw a dynamite stick triggering a feature or to add extra FREE SPINS.
